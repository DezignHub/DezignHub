Project Overview

SynergyFlow was created to address the growing challenges remote teams face in maintaining productivity and effective collaboration across different time zones and work styles.

Through extensive research and iterative design, we developed a solution that combines task management, real-time communication, and workflow automation tailored specifically for distributed teams.

The Challenge

Remote teams often struggle with:

  • Inefficient communication across different time zones

  • Lack of visibility into teammates' workflows and availability

  • Difficulty maintaining focus amidst constant digital distractions

Research & Discovery

User Interviews

Participants

Conducted 25 interviews with remote workers across different industries, team sizes, and time zones to understand pain points and workflows.

Key Findings

82% reported switching between 4+ tools daily, leading to context switching fatigue. 67% felt their team lacked visibility into work progress.

Competitive Analysis

Asana

Strong task management but weak in real-time collaboration features.

Slack

Excellent communication but lacks workflow integration and task tracking.

Toggl

Great time tracking but doesn't address broader collaboration needs.
