BudgetEase

A personal budgeting app designed to simplify financial management through intuitive UX

View Prototype

Project Overview

BudgetEase helps users track expenses, set financial goals, and gain insights into their spending habits with a clean, user-friendly interface.

Challenge

Existing budgeting apps were either too complex or lacked meaningful insights, causing users to abandon them quickly.

Solution

A simplified budgeting experience with visual data, smart categorization, and gentle financial guidance.

Impact

User testing showed 78% improvement in financial awareness and 65% increase in budget adherence.

User Research

Understanding pain points and behaviors was crucial to designing an effective solution.

Key Findings

  • 72% of users abandoned budgeting apps within 2 weeks due to complexity

  • Users wanted "set and forget" functionality with periodic check-ins

  • Visual representations of spending were more effective than raw numbers

  • Users responded better to positive reinforcement than warnings

User Persona

Sarah Miller

28 • Marketing Specialist

Goals

"I want to save for a house but I'm not sure where my money goes each month. I need something simple that doesn't feel like work."

Frustrations

"Other apps make me categorize every transaction manually. I don't have time for that."

Behavior

Checks finances weekly, prefers mobile over desktop, responds well to visual data.

Design Process

Iterative design focused on simplicity, clarity, and actionable insights.
