E-Commerce Platform Redesign

Improving conversion rates through strategic UX enhancements for an eBay-like marketplace

View prototype

Project Overview

The Challenge

The existing platform had a conversion rate below industry standards, with users abandoning purchases due to confusing navigation, overwhelming product pages, and a lengthy checkout process.

Our goal was to redesign the user experience to increase conversion rates by at least 25% while maintaining the platform's core functionality.

Key Objectives

  • Simplify product discovery and comparison
  • Streamline the checkout process
  • Improve trust signals throughout the journey
  • Enhance mobile experience

User Research

User Interviews

Conducted 50+ interviews with both buyers and sellers to understand pain points and expectations.

Analytics Review

Analyzed 6 months of user behavior data to identify drop-off points and navigation patterns.

Competitive Analysis

Benchmarked against 8 leading e-commerce platforms to identify best practices and opportunities.

Key Findings

Information Overload

Product pages contained too much unstructured information, overwhelming users.

Checkout Friction

The 5-step checkout process led to 68% cart abandonment.

Mobile Challenges

Mobile users struggled with small touch targets and slow loading media.

Design Solutions

Streamlined Product Discovery

Redesigned the search and filtering system to help users find relevant products faster. Implemented:

  • Smart search with autocomplete and suggestions
  • Visual filters with real-time results
  • Personalized recommendations based on browsing history

Optimized Product Pages

Restructured product pages to highlight key information and improve scannability:

  • Tabbed information architecture for better organization
  • Sticky "Add to Cart" section for quick access
  • Enhanced media gallery with zoom and 360° view

Simplified Checkout Flow

Reduced the checkout process from 5 steps to 2 while maintaining all necessary information:

  • Guest checkout option prominently displayed
  • Auto-fill for returning customers
  • Multiple payment options with security badges

Key Takeaways

This project demonstrated how thoughtful UX improvements can significantly impact business metrics in e-commerce. The redesign focused on removing friction points while maintaining the platform's robust functionality.

Simplifying complex processes doesn't mean removing features—it means making them more intuitive.

Mobile optimization is no longer optional—it's critical for e-commerce success.

Trust signals (reviews, security badges, clear policies) significantly impact conversion rates.
