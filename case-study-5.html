Voyago Travel App

Streamlining the travel booking experience with intuitive design

UX Design Mobile App 2023
View prototype

Project Overview

A case study on improving the travel booking experience for modern travelers.

The Challenge

Travel booking apps often overwhelm users with too many options and complex navigation. Users reported frustration with finding the best deals and completing bookings quickly.

The Solution

Voyago simplifies the process with a clean interface, personalized recommendations, and a streamlined booking flow that reduces steps by 40% compared to competitors.

My Role

Lead UX Designer responsible for user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

Duration

6 months from discovery to high-fidelity prototype.

DISCOVERY PHASE

Understanding User Pain Points

Through extensive research, we identified key frustrations in existing travel booking experiences.

User Interviews

Conducted 15 interviews with frequent travelers to understand their booking habits and frustrations.

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed 8 competing apps to identify common patterns and opportunities for differentiation.

Survey Data

Collected responses from 200 travelers about their booking preferences and pain points.

Key Findings

Too Many Steps

78% of users abandoned bookings due to lengthy processes

Information Overload

Users felt overwhelmed by irrelevant options and promotions

Lack of Personalization

92% wanted recommendations based on their travel history

Hidden Costs

Unexpected fees were the #1 reason for booking abandonment

DESIGN PROCESS

From Concept to Solution

An iterative approach to solving the identified problems.

Research

Understanding user needs

Wireframing

Low-fidelity concepts

Prototyping

Interactive mockups

Testing

User validation

Information Architecture

We restructured the navigation to prioritize the most common user tasks, reducing the number of taps needed to complete a booking.

User Flow Optimization

By analyzing user paths, we eliminated unnecessary steps and combined related actions to create a more linear booking experience.

THE SOLUTION

Key Features & Improvements

Designed to address the specific pain points we discovered.

FEATURE 01

Personalized Dashboard

A customized home screen showing relevant destinations based on past trips and preferences, with quick access to frequent booking types.

  • Reduced search time by 35%
  • Increased engagement with recommended trips
  • One-tap access to frequent bookings
FEATURE 02

Streamlined Booking Flow

A simplified 4-step process that shows total price upfront and eliminates unnecessary form fields through smart defaults.

  • 40% fewer steps than competitors
  • Clear pricing breakdown from the start
  • Auto-filled information saves time
FEATURE 03

Integrated Trip Management

All trip details in one place with real-time updates, documents storage, and local recommendations based on itinerary.

  • Centralized access to all trip details
  • Push notifications for important updates
  • Contextual local recommendations

REFLECTION

Lessons Learned

Key Takeaways

This project reinforced the importance of thorough user research in identifying real pain points rather than perceived ones. We initially assumed price was the biggest concern, but research revealed that transparency and simplicity were actually more important to users.

Next Steps

Future iterations could explore integrating AI for even more personalized recommendations and developing a web version with synchronized data. There's also potential to expand the trip management features to include collaborative planning for group trips.
