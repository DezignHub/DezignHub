Fintech Mobile App

Redesigning Investments for Millennials

Creating a modern, intuitive investment experience tailored for the next generation of investors

Project Overview

The challenge was to redesign an existing investment app to better serve millennial users who often feel intimidated by traditional financial platforms. Our goal was to simplify complex financial concepts while maintaining the depth of functionality that serious investors need.

Through extensive user research and iterative design, we created an experience that educates while it empowers, helping younger investors feel confident in their financial decisions.

Project Details

Client

FinTech Startup

Role

Lead UX Designer

Duration

6 months

Team

1 designer

Research

Understanding the Problem

Millennials face unique financial challenges and have different expectations from financial tools than previous generations.

Financial Anxiety

78% of millennials reported feeling anxious about investing, with many avoiding it altogether due to complexity.

Mobile-First Mindset

92% of our target users primarily manage finances through mobile apps, expecting seamless, app-native experiences.

Education Gap

Users wanted to learn about investing but found most educational content either too technical or too simplistic.

Design Process

From Sketches to Solution

Step 01

User Flows & Wireframing

We mapped out key user journeys to identify pain points in the existing app. The onboarding process was particularly problematic, with many users dropping off before completing account setup.

  • Simplified account creation from 12 steps to 5
  • Added progressive disclosure for complex features
  • Created contextual help throughout the app
Step 02

Visual Design System

We developed a design system that balanced approachability with the seriousness of financial management. The color palette combined trustworthy blues with energetic accents to create a dynamic yet reliable feel.

The typography system used a clean, geometric sans-serif for readability while maintaining personality through careful hierarchy and spacing.

Step 03

Interactive Prototyping

High-fidelity prototypes allowed us to test microinteractions and transitions that made complex financial data feel approachable. We particularly focused on:

  • Animated data visualizations that explained market movements
  • Gesture-based navigation for quick access to key features
  • Contextual tooltips that appeared when users hesitated
Solution

Key Features of the Redesign

Personalized Onboarding

We transformed the onboarding into a conversational experience that adapted to the user's financial knowledge and goals. Instead of overwhelming forms, users answered simple questions that built their profile over time.

"How much risk are you comfortable with?"

Shown with simple slider and visual indicators rather than technical jargon about volatility and asset allocation.

Smart Dashboard

The home screen dynamically surfaces the most relevant information based on the user's behavior and portfolio. Machine learning helps prioritize what to show, reducing decision fatigue.

  • Performance highlights with plain-language explanations
  • Actionable insights tailored to the user's goals
  • Quick actions for common tasks like deposits

Integrated Learning

Financial education was woven throughout the app with bite-sized lessons that appeared at relevant moments. Users could dive deeper with curated content matched to their portfolio.

Why did my portfolio drop today?

2 min video explaining market fluctuations

Guide to ESG Investing

Tailored to your current holdings
